# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assumptions:
- Average deal size: $2,000 (one purchase of discounted credits)
- Gross margin: 20% (buying at discount, selling at less-than-retail)
- Gross profit per deal: $400
- Repeat purchase rate: 40% within 6 months
- LTV over 12 months: $400 + (40% × $400) = $560

**A converted lead is worth ~$560 LTV to Credex.**

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel

| Channel | Cost | Conversions | CAC |
|---------|------|-------------|-----|
| Hacker News post | $0 | ~50 audits | $0 |
| Reddit posts | $0 | ~30 audits | $0 |
| X/Twitter DMs | $0 (time only) | ~20 audits | $0 |
| Newsletter feature | $500 (paid) | ~200 audits | $2.50/audit |
| Referral program | $20/referral | varies | $20/lead |

At $0 CAC for organic channels, even a 1% conversion from audit → purchase is profitable.

## Conversion Funnel

| Stage | Rate | Numbers (per 1000 visitors) |
|-------|------|----------------------------|
| Visit → Audit completed | 40% | 400 audits |
| Audit completed → Email captured | 20% | 80 leads |
| Email captured → Consultation booked | 15% | 12 consultations |
| Consultation → Credit purchase | 30% | 4 purchases |

**Net: 1000 visitors → 4 purchases → $2,240 revenue → $448 gross profit**

## What Makes This Profitable

- CAC is near $0 for organic channels
- The tool itself is the acquisition — no paid ads needed
- Shareable URLs create a viral loop with zero marginal cost
- Email capture gives Credex a retargeting list for future products

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

**Revenue needed:** $1,000,000 / 12 = $83,333/month

**At $2,000 average deal size:** 42 deals/month needed

**Working backwards:**
- 42 deals/month ÷ 30% close rate = 140 consultations/month
- 140 consultations ÷ 15% booking rate = 933 email leads/month
- 933 leads ÷ 20% capture rate = 4,667 audits/month
- 4,667 audits ÷ 40% completion rate = 11,667 visitors/month

**11,667 visitors/month is achievable by month 6 through:**
- Organic SEO on "AI tool cost" keywords (3–6 month lag)
- Consistent Hacker News and Reddit presence
- Viral sharing of audit result URLs
- 1–2 newsletter features per month

**This is realistic. The math works at modest traffic numbers.**

## Risks

- AI tool pricing changes frequently — audit engine needs monthly updates
- Low-savings audits reduce Credex CTA exposure — need to show value anyway
- Groq API rate limits at scale — need to budget for paid tier at 5k+ audits/day
