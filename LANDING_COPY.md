## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no account, no catch. The audit is free because Credex makes money when we help you find a better deal on AI credits — not by charging you for the audit.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in our audit engine is pulled directly from vendor pricing pages and verified weekly. We cite every source in our pricing data file. If something is wrong, email us and we will fix it within 24 hours.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: We store your tool selections and savings numbers to generate your shareable URL. We never store payment information. If you provide your email, we use it only to send your report and — for high-savings cases — to introduce you to Credex. No spam.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex is a marketplace for discounted AI infrastructure credits. Companies that overforecast their AI usage sell their surplus credits at a discount. Credex connects them with startups that want the same tools for less. The audit tool is free — Credex is the optional next step for teams with significant savings opportunities.

**Q: Why should I trust your recommendations?**
A: Our audit engine uses deterministic rules, not AI guesswork. Every recommendation has a one-sentence reason you can verify yourself. A finance person should read our logic and agree with it. We do not manufacture savings — if your stack is optimal, we will tell you that too.
